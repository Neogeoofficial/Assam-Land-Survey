## 🎯 **Executive Summary**

The **Assam Land Survey Project** represents a **₹500+ Crore state-wide digital transformation initiative** that is fundamentally reshaping land administration across Assam. This comprehensive program combines cutting-edge surveying technology with systematic data collection to create the most accurate and transparent land record system in the state's history.
